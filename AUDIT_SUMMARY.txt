1. PLATFORM OVERVIEW
================================================================================

WHAT IS EMOTIONSCARE?
- Comprehensive emotional wellness platform for B2C and B2B users
- Combines clinical psychology, AI, and immersive experiences
- Privacy-first with RGPD compliance and AES-256-GCM encryption
- WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility certified
- Production-ready with 46 E2E tests, 100+ unit tests

MARKET POSITION:
- B2C: Individual consumers seeking daily emotional wellness
- B2B: Enterprises (HR departments) with employee well-being programs
- Clinical: Integration with mental health professionals
- Therapeutic: Music and VR-based interventions

KEY FEATURES:
✓ Emotional journaling (voice + text with AI analysis)
✓ Breathing exercises with HRV biofeedback
✓ Clinical assessments (WHO-5, GAD-7, PSS-10, PHQ-9)
✓ Therapeutic music generation via Suno AI

✓ Social community features
